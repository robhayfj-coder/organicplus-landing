About

OrganicPlus Fiji

We are an agricultural biotechnology company based in Navua, Fiji. We convert food-production waste into advanced bioactive inputs that improve soil biology and crop performance—without compromising on safety or sustainability.

Vision: Lead high-performance farming innovations in the South Pacific.
Mission: Help farmers unlock higher production and profitability with organic solutions.
Values: Sustainability • Quality • Innovation • Farmer Success

Products

Flagship portfolio

Focus products are below. Additional lines are in active R&D.

Liquid Compost — Harvest Booster

Bio-active nutrient solution that improves soil fertility, boosts yield, and increases resilience to pests & disease.

  • Boosts harvest size & quality
  • Improves soil health & microbial activity
  • Strengthens natural plant defenses
  • Enhances taste & shelf-life

GreenGuard — Crop Protector

Natural insecticide & fungicide from bamboo vinegar that controls pests & disease without harming beneficial microbes.

  • Reduces pest pressure & disease
  • Improves plant vigor and crop health
  • Safe, user-friendly, and eco-conscious
  • Use in rotation with Liquid Compost
