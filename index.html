About OrganicPlus Fiji

OrganicPlus Fiji is an agricultural biotechnology company based in Navua, Fiji. We convert national food-production waste into advanced bioactive inputs that improve soil health, strengthen plant resilience, and increase both the quality and quantity of harvests.

Vision: To be the market leader in high-performance farming innovations in the South Pacific.
Mission: Help farmers unlock higher crop production and profitability through high-performance organic solutions.
Values: Sustainability • Quality • Innovation • Farmer Success

Why Choose OrganicPlus Fiji

We deliver a bio-performance farming system trusted across Fiji. Our proprietary microbial formulations rejuvenate soil, strengthen plant immunity, and increase harvest quality while remaining safe, sustainable, and easy to use.

Product Portfolio

Focus products are below. Additional lines are in active R&D.

Liquid Compost — Harvest Booster

Bio-active nutrient solution that improves soil fertility, boosts yield, and increases resilience to pests & diseases.

  • Boosts harvest size & quality
  • Improves soil health & microbial activity
  • Strengthens natural plant defenses
  • Enhances taste & shelf-life of produce

GreenGuard — Crop Protector

Natural insecticide & fungicide from bamboo vinegar that controls pests & disease without harming beneficial microbes.

  • Reduces pest pressure & disease outbreaks
  • Improves plant vigor and crop health
  • Safe, user-friendly, and eco-conscious
  • Use in rotation with Liquid Compost
