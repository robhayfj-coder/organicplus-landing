About OrganicPlus

We’re an agricultural biotechnology company serving the South Pacific. We develop advanced bioactive inputs that improve soil biology, strengthen plant resilience, and increase both the quality and quantity of harvests so that farmers improve their profitability.

Vision: Lead high-performance farming innovations in the South Pacific.
Mission: We commit to the development of high-quality farming solutions that help farmers improve crop productivity and profitability. We achieve this with an unrivalled commitment to continuous innovation in the biotech space.
Values: Sustainability • Quality • Innovation • Farmer Success
