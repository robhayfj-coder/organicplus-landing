About OrganicPlus Fiji

OrganicPlus Fiji is an agricultural biotechnology company based in Navua, Fiji. We convert national food-production waste into advanced bioactive inputs that improve soil health, strengthen plant resilience, and increase harvest quality and quantity.

Vision: To be the market leader in high-performance farming innovations in the South Pacific.
Mission: Help farmers unlock higher crop production and profitability through high-performance organic solutions.
Values: Sustainability • Quality • Innovation • Farmer Success

Why Choose OrganicPlus Fiji

We deliver a bio-performance farming system trusted across Fiji. Our proprietary microbial formulations rejuvenate soil, strengthen plant immunity, and increase harvest quality while remaining safe, sustainable, and easy to use.

Letter from the Director

At OrganicPlus Fiji, we are driven by one purpose: to empower South Pacific farmers with high-performance farming innovations that deliver measurable, long-term impact. Our products are engineered biological solutions designed to enhance productivity, strengthen resilience, and improve profitability across Fiji’s farming communities.

Since inception, we have focused on results. By combining biotechnology and sustainable practices, we have helped farmers increase yields, improve soil fertility, and produce healthier, longer-lasting crops. Through partnerships with Post Fiji, the Ministry of Agriculture, and others, we are making proven, locally manufactured solutions accessible to farmers nationwide.

We remain committed to continuous innovation, research, and expansion — ensuring Fiji and the wider Pacific have access to world-class agricultural solutions made right here at home.

Rob Hay
Director, Organic Plus (Fiji) Pte Ltd
